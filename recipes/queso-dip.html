Queso Dip

Description

It's famous for a reason. This game-day queso dip is not only a creamy, delicious appetizer-it may be one of the best reasons for football to exist.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package VELVEETA, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 (10 ounce) can Ro Tel Original Diced Tomatoes & Green Chiles, undrained

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in microwave bowl.
  2. Microwave on HIGH 5 min. or until VELVEETA is completely melted and mixture is well blended, stirring after 3 min.
